GNSS/GPS Equipment

High-precision satellite positioning systems for centimeter-level accuracy in all survey applications.

  • Static GNSS Systems

Total Stations

Survey Drones (UAVs)

Professional unmanned aerial vehicles for rapid aerial mapping and data collection.

  • LiDAR Systems

Hydrographic Equipment

Specialized instruments for underwater terrain mapping and bathymetric surveys.

  • Single Beam Echo Sounders
  • Multi-Beam Systems
  • Acoustic Doppler Current Profilers
  • Side-Scan Sonar

Depth Range: 0.5m to 200m+

GIS Software Suite

Industry-leading software for spatial analysis, mapping, and geodatabase management.

  • ArcGIS Pro & ArcMap
  • QGIS Open Source
  • AutoCAD Civil 3D
  • Global Mapper

Capabilities: 2D/3D mapping, analysis, modeling

Photogrammetry Systems

Advanced processing software for creating 3D models from aerial and terrestrial imagery.

  • DJI Terra

  • Pix4Dmapper
