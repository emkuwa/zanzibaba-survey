Our Process

Our Topographical Survey Process

01

Site Assessment

We visit your site to understand the project requirements, assess terrain conditions, and identify any challenges. This helps us plan the most efficient survey approach.

02

Control Establishment

Precise survey control points are established using GNSS RTK technology, ensuring accurate positioning throughout the survey area.

03

Data Collection

Using total stations, GNSS receivers, and optionally drones, we systematically collect topographic data including contours, spot heights, and feature locations.

04

Processing & Analysis

Collected data is processed using advanced software to create digital terrain models, contour maps, and cross-sections.

05

Quality Control

Rigorous quality checks ensure accuracy and completeness. We verify data against known control points and conduct field checks.

06

Delivery

You receive comprehensive survey deliverables including CAD drawings, digital terrain models, contour plans, and a detailed report.

Equipment Used for Topographical Surveys

Robotic Total Stations

Automated target recognition for efficient detail surveys. Measures angles and distances with high precision.

Survey Drones

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a topographical survey?

A topographical survey is a detailed mapping of natural and man-made features of a piece of land. It shows elevation changes, contours, existing structures, boundaries, drainage patterns, vegetation, and other physical characteristics of the terrain. This information is essential for architects, engineers, and planners to design projects that work with the existing site conditions.

How long does a topographical survey take in Zanzibar?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the area. A standard residential plot (500-1000 sqm) typically takes 1-2 days. Larger commercial or industrial sites may take 3-7 days. We provide accurate time estimates after initial site assessment.

How much does a topographical survey cost in Zanzibar?

Topographical survey costs in Zanzibar vary based on plot size, terrain complexity, and detail required. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific project requirements.

What equipment do you use for topographical surveys?

We use state-of-the-art equipment including GNSS RTK receivers, robotic total stations, drone photogrammetry systems, and 3D laser scanners to ensure centimeter-level accuracy in all our topographical surveys.

Do I need a topographical survey for building permission in Zanzibar?

Yes, most building permit applications in Zanzibar require a topographical survey showing the site's existing conditions, boundaries, and proposed development. We ensure your survey meets all local authority requirements.

What deliverables do I receive from a topographical survey?

Deliverables typically include contour plans, digital terrain models (DTM), topographic maps in CAD/GIS formats, cross-sections, volume calculations, and a comprehensive survey report.

Can you do topographical surveys on remote islands in Zanzibar?

Yes, we provide topographical survey services throughout the Zanzibar Archipelago including Pemba Island, Mafia Island, and smaller surrounding islands. We have experience operating in remote and challenging terrain.

What is the accuracy of your topographical surveys?

Our topographical surveys achieve centimeter-level accuracy using RTK GNSS and total station technology. For large-scale projects, we use drone photogrammetry with ground control points to ensure consistent accuracy across the entire survey area.

Do you provide topographical surveys for coastal areas?

Yes, we specialize in coastal topographical surveys including beach profiling, shoreline mapping, and coastal erosion studies. We combine land survey techniques with hydrographic methods for comprehensive coastal zone mapping.
