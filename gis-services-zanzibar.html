Our GIS Project Process

01

Requirements Analysis

We meet with your team to understand project objectives, data requirements, desired outputs, and how the GIS solution will support your decision-making processes.

02

Data Assessment

Evaluate existing spatial data, identify gaps, determine data collection needs, and establish data quality standards for the project.

03

Data Collection & Processing

Gather field data using GPS receivers, process satellite imagery, digitize existing maps, and integrate data from multiple sources into a unified database.

Database Design

Design and build geodatabases with appropriate structure, relationships, and metadata to ensure efficient data storage and retrieval.

05

Analysis & Modeling

Perform spatial analysis, overlay operations, proximity analysis, and predictive modeling to generate actionable insights from your data.

06

Visualization & Delivery

Create professional maps, interactive web applications, and comprehensive reports. Deliver all data in standard formats with full documentation.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GIS and how can it help my project?

GIS (Geographic Information System) is a technology that captures, stores, analyzes, and presents spatial and geographic data. It helps projects by enabling better decision-making through visual mapping, spatial analysis, and data integration. GIS is essential for urban planning, environmental management, infrastructure development, and land administration.

What GIS services do you offer in Zanzibar?

We offer comprehensive GIS services including spatial analysis, custom map creation, land use planning, geodatabase design, environmental impact assessment support, urban planning support, infrastructure mapping, asset management systems, and GIS consulting for government and private sector projects.

How much do GIS services cost in Zanzibar?

GIS service costs vary based on project scope, data requirements, and complexity. Simple mapping projects may start from a few hundred dollars, while comprehensive spatial analysis or geodatabase projects can be several thousand. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

What software do you use for GIS?

We use industry-standard GIS software including ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Google Earth Pro, Global Mapper, and specialized tools for remote sensing analysis. We also develop custom web mapping applications using platforms like Mapbox, Leaflet, and OpenLayers.

Can you create custom maps for my project?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom maps tailored to your specific requirements. Whether you need thematic maps, planning maps, environmental maps, or presentation-quality maps, we design and produce professional cartographic products in both print and digital formats.

Do you provide GIS consulting for government projects?

Absolutely. We work extensively with government agencies in Zanzibar and Tanzania on urban planning, land administration, environmental management, and infrastructure projects. Our team understands local regulatory requirements and data standards.

What data formats can you work with?

We work with all standard GIS data formats including shapefiles, GeoJSON, GeoTIFF, KML/KMX, DXF/DWG, and database formats like PostgreSQL/PostGIS. We can also import data from spreadsheets, CSV files, and other common formats for integration into GIS projects.

Can you integrate survey data with GIS?

Yes, integrating survey data with GIS is one of our core strengths. We combine topographical surveys, cadastral data, drone imagery, and field measurements into comprehensive GIS databases for complete project visualization and analysis.

How long does a GIS project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope. Simple map creation may take 2-5 days, while comprehensive spatial analysis or geodatabase development can take 2-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after initial project assessment.

Do you offer GIS training?

Yes, we offer GIS training workshops and customized training programs for organizations looking to build internal GIS capacity. Training covers ArcGIS, QGIS, data collection techniques, and GIS best practices tailored to your sector.
